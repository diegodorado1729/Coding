- Si queremos cambiar el indice agregamos el parametro `index` dentro del parentesis:
`pd.DataFrame({'Bob': ['I liked it.', 'It was awful.'], 'Sue': ['Pretty good.', 'Bland.']}, index=['Product A', 'Product B'])`
